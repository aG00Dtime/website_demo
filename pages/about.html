About us

Eden Island Villa Resort

Eden Island has metamorphosed from one resort in Saidia, Morocco, to become one of the most prominent hospitality names in the world of vacation. With the new venture into the caribbean, with Jamaica being a popular vacation destination, Eden is currently a major competitor in the Caribbean vacation experiences and entend to be vehemently committed to bringing the mediterranean culture to the region, giving the caribbean people a taste of the delightful mediterranean lifestyle.

Over the years, Eden has gained its notoriety as one of most romantic resort experiences for couples offering astonishing beachfront scenes, opulent accommodations and many other features that make Eden an experience of a lifetime.

Couples come to get away from the city and reconnect with nature and with each other. Our Resort is a year-round retreat built from the ground up with visitor needs in mind. Our sprawling five acre property is located on a private island.

This adult only resort was created to simulate and relax the mind and take away the stress of the everyday world from check in to check out. The luxury resort has recently added a new location along the beautiful sunkissed tropical and exquisite beach in Kingson, Jamaica. The island features activities such as gourmet dining, unique bars, on the beach cinemas and live entertainment, just to list a few and also cater for honeymoons and romantic getaways.
